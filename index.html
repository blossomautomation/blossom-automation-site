A real engagement

The dashboard said 47.
She said 12.

47

Calls logged  vs.  12 actual

Amanda runs operations at Work Shield. She'd pulled up the KPI dashboard before we got there — activity by rep, calls logged, contacts touched. The numbers looked fine. We asked her coordinator the same questions directly. The coordinator's version of last week didn't match the dashboard's version by a factor of four.

We stopped looking at the dashboard. We asked the coordinator to walk us through her morning — what she opens, what she fills in, where she looks when she needs something. By the third sentence, we both saw it. The contact form had two fields that captured the same information. Nobody had told her which one the reports were pulling from. She'd been logging into the wrong one for months.

The CRM wasn't broken. The workflow wasn't broken. The gap was one sentence nobody had said out loud.

Most CRM problems aren't technical. They're the conversation nobody had before the system went live.
