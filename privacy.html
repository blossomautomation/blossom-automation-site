Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect information when you work with us or visit this site.

Last updated: May 2026

Who we are

Blossom Automation is a CRM consulting firm based in Austin, Texas. We help small businesses and nonprofits build systems they can actually use. This policy applies to information collected through this website (blossomautomation.com), RootCheck (rootcheck.blossomautomation.com), and any direct communications with us.

Questions about this policy can be directed to oscar@blossomautomation.com.

Information we collect

Information you provide directly

When you reach out through the contact form, book a call, or subscribe to our newsletter, you provide:

  • Name and email address
  • Company name and role (if applicable)
  • Any information you share in your message

We use this information to respond to you, schedule conversations, and deliver the newsletter you signed up for. Nothing else.

Information collected automatically

When you visit this site, standard web analytics tools collect basic information about your visit:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URL (how you found us)
  • Browser type and device
  • General geographic location (country or city level)

This site uses Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our content. GA4 data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. You can opt out of GA4 tracking using Google's opt-out browser add-on.

RootCheck

RootCheck is a HubSpot portal audit tool. When you connect your HubSpot account, RootCheck accesses your portal data to run the audit and generate a score. We do not store your HubSpot credentials. Audit results are associated with your email address so we can follow up. We do not share or sell your portal data with anyone.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and schedule conversations
  • Deliver the newsletter you requested
  • Improve this website and our services based on aggregate usage patterns
  • Fulfill any consulting engagement you've hired us for

We do not sell your information. We do not share it with third parties except for the service providers that help us operate (see below).

Third-party services

We use a small number of tools to run this business:

  • HubSpot — CRM and contact management. Information you submit through our forms goes into HubSpot. HubSpot Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics 4 — Website analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — Database infrastructure for RootCheck. Supabase Privacy Policy

Each of these providers has their own privacy policies. We choose vendors that take data handling seriously, but we don't control their practices.

Cookies

This site uses cookies primarily for analytics (Google Analytics). Cookies are small files stored in your browser that help us understand how you're using the site. You can control cookie behavior through your browser settings or the Google Analytics opt-out tool linked above.

We don't use cookies for advertising or to track you across other websites.

Data retention

We keep contact information and correspondence for as long as the relationship is active and for a reasonable period afterward. If you'd like us to delete your information, reach out and we'll do it.

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months by default, consistent with GA4 defaults.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal information. If you want to exercise any of these rights, email us. We'll respond within a reasonable time.

If you're in the European Union or California, you have specific additional rights under GDPR and CCPA respectively. We're a small firm and we'll work with you directly.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information we handle. Our tools use industry-standard encryption in transit. No system is perfectly secure, but we don't treat data carelessly.

Changes to this policy

If we make meaningful changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top. Material changes will be noted clearly. We don't anticipate frequent updates.

Questions

If anything here is unclear or you'd like to know something this document doesn't cover, email us at oscar@blossomautomation.com. We'll respond directly.
